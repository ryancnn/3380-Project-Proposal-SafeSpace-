INSTRUCTIONS
1.	Go to https://docs.flutter.dev/get-started/install
2.	Follow the steps to install flutter on your computer
3.	After installing flutter, open your preferred text editor(visual studio is recommended)
4.	Plug in an android device using a USB cable.
5.	In the visual studio terminal, type flutter run and wait for the installation to complete.
6.	Once complete, the app should be opened on your phone.
7.	With the app open, press register and create an account. After clicking create you are brought to the main page.
8.	Go to the explore tab and join a group.
9.	Send a message in the group.
